Mission and Vision

Neuron Fire mission and vission is to help students with personalized academic and character education approach while avoiding one to many student approaach using Artificial Intelligence and Machine LEarning for student grade predictions. AI course suggestions, dashboards for 360 degrees visibility of student skillset to help teachers and mentors while assisting students.
