About Us

In 2021, Neuron Fire got started as youtube channel to promote growth mindset through animation concepts to develop strong character for children. Now through Build with AI hackathon 2021 it is expanding its capabilities to Artificial and machine learning to help students in academic and character education.

  • Mission: To help students with personalized academic and character education approach while avoiding one to many students approach using AI and ML predictions and recommendations to make student more productive and successful.

  • Vision: To promote growth mindset to children at early ages and help students build strong character along with excellent academic scores.

  • How it works: while closing the gaps among students, teachers and parents to Improve interactivity and learning curve of students.

  • • Predicts student grades based on student previous data.
  • • AI course suggestions based on grades and student check in forms.
  • • Dashboards for 360 degrees visibility of student skill set
  • • Student-Teacher-Parent check in Surveys
  • • Lifestyle Habits check-in to predict mental health of students
  • • Student self discipline and commitment prediction based on self study vs group study no of hours.
